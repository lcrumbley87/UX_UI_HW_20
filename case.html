    S.O.S for Humanitarian Actions

    About

    My team and I were tasked with redesigning the SOS for Humanitarian Actions website. SOS for Humanitarian Actions is a non-profit organization based in the Atlanta. The organization workes with local homeless shelters as well as doing charitable work on in need areas of Togo, Africa. Our goal was to redesign the existing website to give a more professional and trustworthy appearence to increase donations.

    Design Process

    We were given 3 weeks to choose an organization and bring the new design to a high fidelity clickable prototype. We used a combination of tools such as Miro, Figma, interviews and testing to complete our goal.

    Last updated 3 mins ago

    Research

    I started off by conducting 5 user test on the original site to recieve feedback and pin point users pain points. I gave each user 3 tasks to complete while on the site. My team recorded the findings and took notes.

    Last updated 3 mins ago

    Definition

    My team and I took our findings and sorted them using an affinity diagram. We then grouped all the information into different catagories and placed them into a priortization matrix.

    Last updated 3 mins ago

    Ideation

    We created a user persona to personalize our users experience to help us empathise and understand them. We brainstormed ideas to help with the pain points found from the first round of testing/ interviews.

    Last updated 3 mins ago

    Prototyping

    We started with redling the existing homepage with data gathered from testing to easily visualize some of the pain points. We then created a low fidelity prototype to start us in the right direction. after conducting another set of 5 user test we recorded our findings in notes and immediatly created our mid fidelity clickable prototype

    Last updated 3 mins ago

    Testing

    After conducting 5 user tests we were able to really bring our webpage to its full professional level. I redesigned the logo with the two flags of Georgia and Togo and redesigned the donations page to maximize ease of use. Along with some other visual changes my team and I worked on together we were ready to present of project.
